About us

The main purpose of this project is to help the public in knowing their place details and getting their problems solved in online without going to the officer regularly until the problem is solved, By this system the public can save his time and eradicate corruption in government offices. Its main purpose is to provide a smart and easy way through web Application for Complaint registration and its tracking and thus to prevent Corruption. This application for complaint management system where public can register complaints for street light, water pipe leakage, rain water drainage & road reconstruction
